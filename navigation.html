Guide to navigating the project

This story is best navigated on a desktop monitor.

"Lastliving.html" is a linear narrative. Within each page is always a link or a url to progress. You can return to previous pages by clicking the "back" button on your browser, or returning to this page for the "Table of Contents".

At certain points, this site contains embedded games. To take control of the game, click on the square where the game is being displayed.

This site also contains external links to pages that I have not created myself.
